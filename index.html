Reflection & Goals

Self-Reflection on My Performance This Year

A Year of Learning and Growth

This year at ASU has been one of the most transformative times in my career. I've learned so much—technically, academically, and personally. Looking back at when I first joined, I can honestly say I feel proud of how far I've come.

Inspired by a Productive Environment

I feel incredibly fortunate to be part of a team where everyone is passionate and focused on their work. The energy here is contagious—it motivates me every day. My professor has been extremely supportive, and the overall environment helps me stay creative and driven.

Building My Own Ideas into Reality

I've started developing some of my own apps, including the AI River Analyzer Morphology Analyzer. It's exciting to see my ideas take shape and move closer to becoming real tools that others can use.

Gaining Confidence in My Path

This year gave me clarity. I now feel more confident in my skills, more sure of the kind of research I want to do, and excited about the tools I'm building. I'm really happy I chose this path—it's already making a difference.

Career Goals Statement

Research That Makes an Impact

I want to keep working at the intersection of geospatial science, AI, and environmental research. My goal is to create tools and insights that can actually help communities and researchers understand complex systems like rivers and climate.

Finish and Launch My Own Tools

I'm currently working on apps that are close to my heart—especially the River Analyzer. In the next phase of my journey, I want to polish and launch these tools so they can be used in real-world scenarios.

Write and Share What I'm Learning

I'm working on research papers that reflect the projects and challenges I've been tackling. Publishing these will help me contribute back to the research community and start meaningful conversations.

Keep Growing and Collaborating

Long-term, I want to lead and collaborate on interdisciplinary projects that combine science, tech, and design. I'm excited to keep learning, mentoring others, and building solutions that really matter.
